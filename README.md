# 🏛️ Age of History: European Theater 1936

An immersive grand strategy game inspired by Age of History, set in the tumultuous period of 1936 Europe on the brink of World War II.

## 🌟 Features

### 🗺️ Enhanced Map System
- **Larger, more detailed map** with improved zoom and interaction
- **Territory-based gameplay** with clickable regions
- **Real-time unit visualization** on the map
- **Enhanced visual effects** with smooth animations

### 🏛️ Country Management System
- **Detailed country information panels** with comprehensive statistics
- **Historical leaders and flags** for authentic 1936 atmosphere
- **Government system changes** - Transform your nation's political structure
- **Economic and military statistics** with visual progress bars

### 🌍 Massive Soviet Union
- **Historically accurate USSR** spanning from Eastern Europe to Siberia
- **Multiple Russian territories** divided into strategic regions
- **Extensive Soviet satellite states** and republics
- **Realistic territory distribution** for 1936 geopolitical situation

### 🤖 Advanced AI System
- **Intelligent AI personalities** (Aggressive, Defensive, Balanced, Expansionist)
- **Strategic decision making** based on country strength and opportunities
- **Realistic diplomatic behavior** with neighbor analysis
- **Dynamic war declarations** and territorial expansion

### 🏛️ Government Systems
Transform your nation with different government types:
- **🗳️ Democracy** - Increased stability, reduced war support
- **👑 Monarchy** - Balanced approach with diplomatic bonuses
- **⚡ Fascism** - Enhanced military power, reduced stability
- **☭ Communism** - Industrial development and economic growth

### 📊 Advanced Statistics
- **Stability** - Internal political cohesion
- **War Support** - Population's willingness to fight
- **Industry** - Economic and production capacity
- **Military** - Armed forces effectiveness
- **Population** - Total national population
- **Power Level** - Overall national strength classification

### 🎮 Age of History-Style Interface
- **Authentic medieval/classical styling** with golden accents
- **Immersive sound effects** and visual feedback
- **Modal-based information system** for detailed country data
- **Professional UI animations** and transitions

## 🎯 How to Play

### Getting Started
1. **Enter your leader name** on the welcome screen
2. **Select your nation** from the available countries
3. **Begin your conquest** of Europe in 1936

### Basic Gameplay
- **Click on territories** to view country information
- **Right-click or Ctrl+click** for detailed country panels
- **Declare war** on neighboring nations
- **Buy military units** to strengthen your forces
- **Advance turns** to progress through time

### Advanced Features
- **Change government types** to gain different bonuses
- **Monitor national statistics** for strategic planning
- **Engage in complex diplomacy** with AI nations
- **Expand your territory** through strategic warfare

## 🔧 Customization

The game is highly modular and can be easily customized:

1. **Add new countries** by editing the `countriesData` object
2. **Modify AI behavior** through personality settings
3. **Add new government types** in the government system
4. **Customize visual themes** via CSS variables
5. **Extend victory conditions** in the game logic
